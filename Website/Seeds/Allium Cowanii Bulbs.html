Allium Cowanii Bulbs



₹ 195 250 gm




One of the easiest early-summer flowers, the allium Cowanii can be your own personal snow patch with its milk white blooms. It has loosely packed star-shaped flowers with grey-green foliage that is a great addition to any home garden. This fast-growing varity loves full sun and a well draining soil, either in a container or a soil bed.
